The luxury industry is data-rich and intelligence-poor. Brands like Dior, Chanel, and Hermès sit on years of clienteling records, VIP purchase histories, and supply chain data — yet most decisions still rely on intuition rather than intelligence.


I am building systems that change this — combining classical machine learning, deep learning on sequential data, unsupervised network analysis, and Generative AI into platforms designed specifically for the constraints and values of luxury retail: exclusivity, personalisation, and brand integrity.


My work is informed by the full stack of modern AI — from XGBoost fraud classifiers to BERT fine-tuned on brand documentation, from LSTM time-series anomaly detection to LangGraph autonomous agents that sweep resale platforms for threats.
